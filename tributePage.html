ALAN TURING

Father of Modern Computer Science

Here's the timeline of Alan Turing's life

  • 1912: Born on 23 June 1912
  • 1934: Graduated from King's College, Cambridge
  • 1936: Published his paper "On Comptuable Numbers, with an application to Entscheidungsproblem"
  • 1938: Began part time working at the Government Code And Cipher School. And started cracking the Enigma code.
  • 1948: Turing was appointed as the reader in the Mathematics department of Victoria College, Manchester
  • 1949: Become the Deputy Director of Computing Machine Laboratory,where he worked on software for Manchester 1.
  • 1952: Turing was prosecuted for homosexual acts.
  • 1954: Died on 7 June 1954
  • 2013: Queen Elizabeth II granted posthumous pardon

"Those who can imagine anything can create the impossible" -- Alan Turing
